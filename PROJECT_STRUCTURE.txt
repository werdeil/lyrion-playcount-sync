COMPLETE WORKFLOW

1. SyncDetector.find_missing_in_alternative()
   ↓ Discovers missing tracks

2. TrackMatcher.find_best_matches(track)
   ↓ Finds matching suggestions

3. show_match_dialog(parent, track, suggestions, ...)
   ↓ Displays MatchDialog

4. User selects and clicks "Apply"
   ↓ Callback on_apply() executed

5. SyncOperation.to_sql()
   ↓ Generates UPDATE/DELETE queries

6. Database updated
   ↓ Success

7. MainWindow refreshed
   ↓ User sees changes
